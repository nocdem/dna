Legal & Compliance

DNA Protocol operates with transparency, zero data collection, and open source licensing. This page outlines the legal framework governing the protocol, token, and software.

1. Jurisdiction

DNA Protocol operates under the laws of the European Union. All services provided through cpunk.io and associated platforms are subject to EU regulations, including applicable data protection, consumer protection, and digital asset frameworks.

2. Nature of CPUNK Token

CPUNK is a CF20 utility token on the Cellframe blockchain. It is not a security, investment vehicle, or financial instrument of any kind.

The token is used for:

  • Governance voting — participate in protocol decisions through on-chain proposals
  • Network services — DNA identity registration, DHT node operations, and in-app transfers
  • Community participation — feed and wall access, in-chat transfers, and ecosystem alignment

3. No Investment Advice

Nothing on cpunk.io or any associated platform constitutes investment, financial, or legal advice. Cryptocurrency and digital assets involve significant risk, including the possibility of total loss.

Users should conduct their own research and consult with qualified professionals before making any decisions regarding digital assets. Past performance does not indicate future results.

4. Privacy & Data

DNA Protocol collects no personal data. The protocol is designed for zero-knowledge operation from the ground up.

  • No tracking, no analytics, no cookies
  • No phone numbers or email addresses required
  • No telemetry, fingerprinting, or behavioral profiling
  • No centralized servers storing user data

Users control their own cryptographic keys and identity. Privacy is enforced through post-quantum cryptography, not policy documents. There is nothing to leak because nothing is collected.

5. Open Source Licensing

DNA Protocol uses a dual-license model (Copyright 2024–2026 cpunk.io). Core infrastructure is fully open source under Apache 2.0. The application is source-available for security auditing and transparency.

Apache License 2.0 — open source, free to use, modify, and distribute:

  • DNA Connect C Library — core messaging engine, CLI tools, JNI bindings
  • DNA Nodus Server + Client SDK — Kademlia DHT, cluster replication, TCP/UDP transport
  • Shared Crypto Primitives — ML-KEM-1024 (Kyber), ML-DSA-87 (Dilithium), SHA3-512, AES-256-GCM, BIP39
  • CPUNK Web (cpunk.io) — website and public-facing resources

The Apache 2.0 license permits use, modification, distribution, and sublicensing — including commercial use — provided the license and copyright notice are preserved. Full license text: apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0

Source-Available License — DNA Connect desktop and mobile application:

  • Source code is publicly viewable for auditing and security review
  • Bug reports, feature requests, and contributed patches are welcome
  • Copying, modification, distribution, or commercial use of the application code is not permitted
  • Licensing inquiries: licensing@cpunk.io

6. Software Disclaimer

All DNA Protocol software is provided "AS IS", without warranty of any kind, express or implied, including but not limited to the warranties of merchantability, fitness for a particular purpose, and noninfringement.

  • The software is under active development and may contain bugs
  • The project is not responsible for loss of funds, data, or access
  • Users are solely responsible for securing their seed phrases and private keys
  • Lost seed phrases cannot be recovered by anyone — there are no backdoors or master keys

7. Network Disclaimer

The Nodus network is decentralized and community-operated. No single entity controls, manages, or guarantees the availability of the network.

  • No guarantee of uptime, message delivery, or data persistence
  • Stored data has a 7-day TTL (time-to-live) and may expire
  • Network performance depends on the number and health of participating nodes
  • Routing is dynamic, peer-selected, and operator-controlled
