What is DNA Identity?

Revolutionary approach to decentralized digital identity

🔐 DNA: Distributed Naming & Addressing

DNA is CPUNK's core identity protocol that provides unique, verifiable digital identities tied to your Cellframe blockchain wallet. Unlike traditional identity systems controlled by centralized authorities, DNA gives you complete ownership and control over your digital identity.

Key Features:

  • Unique Identity: One DNA name per wallet address
  • Quantum-Safe: Protected by post-quantum cryptography
  • Decentralized: No central authority required
  • Verifiable: Cryptographically provable ownership
  • Portable: Works across all CPUNK services
  • Permanent: Stored immutably on blockchain

Use Cases:

  • Social Identity: Verified profiles in communities
  • Messaging: Secure peer-to-peer communication
  • Authentication: Login without passwords
  • Reputation: Build trust through verified actions
  • Governance: Participate in DAO voting
  • Commerce: Trustless transactions
