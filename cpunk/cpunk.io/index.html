The DNA Suite

Three utilities, one app

DNA-Messenger combines post-quantum messaging, a multi-chain wallet, and decentralized identity into a single client — no phone numbers, no central servers, no compromises.

Messaging

Post-quantum encrypted conversations over a fully decentralized DHT network. No central servers, no metadata collection — only 20 bytes of routing header. Offline messages are held in distributed mailboxes with 7-day TTL until the recipient comes online.

  • E2E encrypted with Kyber1024 + AES-256-GCM
  • 1-to-1 chats, group channels, and feeds
  • Offline delivery — no always-on requirement

Multi-Wallet & In-Chat Transfers

Manage multiple Cellframe wallets directly inside the messenger. Send and receive tokens within any conversation — no need to switch apps or copy-paste addresses.

  • Multi-chain wallet built into the client
  • Transfer funds to any DNA contact in-chat
  • Transaction signing secured by post-quantum keys

Identity Without Personal Data

Your DNA name is your identity — tied to a cryptographic key pair, not to a phone number, email, or any personal information. Register once on-chain and you are reachable across the entire network.

  • No phone number, email, or account required
  • Identity anchored to your wallet public key
  • Portable across all DNA-compatible services
