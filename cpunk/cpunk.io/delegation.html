Overview

What is CELL delegation?

Delegation connects token holders, validators, and protocol security into a single mechanism that aligns incentives and strengthens the network.

In a Proof-of-Stake-style system, validators secure the network by producing blocks, validating transactions, and participating in consensus. Delegation allows CELL holders to support these validators by temporarily assigning stake to them.

What We Do

We operate professional masternodes using tokens delegated by our community. When you delegate your tokens to us, we use them to run secure, high-performance masternodes across multiple networks.

All hardware setup, maintenance, monitoring, and technical operations are handled by our team - you simply delegate and earn rewards.

Reward Sharing

We share masternode earnings with our delegators, keeping only what's needed to cover operational costs and continue improving our infrastructure.

Delegators can earn up to 80% of masternode rewards, with the exact percentage depending on the amount delegated, network conditions, and delegation duration.

Incentives

Why delegate CELL to CPUNK validators?

Delegation is more than an economic mechanism. It is a way to express which infrastructure you trust and want to see grow.

Security For the network

Strengthen network security

More delegated CELL to honest validators increases the economic cost of attacking the network. This directly reinforces the security of both CPUNK and the underlying post-quantum infrastructure.

Rewards For delegators

Earn validation rewards

Validator nodes share a portion of their block or validation rewards with delegators. This aligns the incentives of node operators and token holders who support them.

Community Participation

Support community infrastructure

Many CPUNK-aligned validators are community-run. Delegating to them helps fund long-term infrastructure, tooling, and development efforts around quantum-safe identity and messaging.

Decentralization Distribution

Promote decentralization

A diverse set of well-managed validators prevents concentration of power. When number of validating nodes increase, the more decentralized our voting power becomes.

Signal Alignment

Express your preferences

Choosing to delegate to CPUNK validators is a signal of trust. Together we are making our chain and our investment stronger.

Future Integration

Integration with identity

Over time, delegation can be linked with DNA Identity and governance, enabling identity-aware staking dashboards and community coordination mechanisms.
