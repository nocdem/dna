Strategic Roadmap

CPUNK Protocol’s journey from foundation to global-scale, post-quantum identity and communication infrastructure.

Version 2.1 – Post-quantum decentralized identity & messaging. Focus on DNA, Messenger, DNA Board, governance & ecosystem growth.

📋 Executive Summary

CPUNK Protocol is evolving from a quantum-safe meme coin into a comprehensive post-quantum decentralized identity and communication infrastructure. This roadmap outlines our strategic vision from token launch through full protocol maturity, encompassing identity systems, encrypted communication, decentralized governance, and ecosystem expansion.

Mission: Build the world's first post-quantum identity and communication protocol that empowers users with sovereign digital identity, secure communication, and decentralized governance.

Vision: Become the foundational identity layer for the post-quantum era, enabling billions of users to interact securely across digital platforms without centralized intermediaries.

Phase 3: Expansion (Q2–Q4 2026) 📋 PLANNED

Timeline: April – December 2026 | Status: Planning

Focus: Social platform, voice/video, full decentralization

Major Initiatives:
1. DNA Board – Censorship-Resistant Social Media (Phase 10)

Timeline: 12 weeks | Status: Planning complete

  • No censorship – content cannot be removed (no deletion endpoint)
  • PoH required to post – only verified humans (PoH ≥70) can create posts
  • Open responses – anyone can reply (enables free speech)
  • Community voting – thumbs up/down for quality surfacing
  • Burn economics – all fees permanently burned (deflationary)
  • Self-healing – 3-validator replication with auto-recovery
2. Post-Quantum Voice/Video Calls (Phase 11)

Timeline: ~20 weeks | Status: Research & design complete

  • Kyber512 key exchange via DNA messaging
  • SRTP media with AES-256-GCM encryption
  • NAT traversal (libnice ICE/STUN/TURN)
  • Opus audio codec (48 kHz stereo)
  • VP8/H.264 video codec
3. Full P2P Decentralization ✅ COMPLETE
  • ✅ Local cache & sync (SQLite encrypted with PQ crypto)
  • ✅ Distributed DHT keyserver (replaced centralized HTTP)
  • ✅ Multi-device synchronization via DHT
  • ✅ Background sync protocol
4. Proof of Humanity System
  • Community vouching (peer verification)
  • Zero-knowledge proofs (privacy-preserving)
