Official Contact overview

CPUNK is not a typical company – it is an open ecosystem. Some topics are best handled in public channels, others privately.

Use Telegram and X when you want to join the community conversation, ask general questions, or follow updates.

Use email for partnerships, listings, media, and security disclosures that should not be shared publicly.

If you are unsure which channel to use, start with Telegram – the team or community can redirect you.

General & partnerships

For exchange listings, integrations, collaborations, and partnership proposals, please contact us via email with a short description of:

  • Who you are and which organization you represent
  • What type of collaboration you are interested in
  • Any relevant links or supporting material
General / Partnerships:  info@cpunk.io

Please avoid sending sensitive personal data by email. For strictly confidential topics, mention this clearly so the team can suggest a more appropriate channel.

Security & responsible disclosure

If you believe you have found a security issue in CPUNK, DNA-Messenger, or related infrastructure, we ask that you disclose it responsibly.

  • Include a clear description of the issue and potential impact
  • Provide step-by-step instructions or proof-of-concept, if possible
  • Do not publicly share the vulnerability before it is mitigated
Security / Disclosure:  security@cpunk.io

At this stage we do not promise formal bug bounties, but good-faith researchers are highly appreciated and may be recognized by the community.

Impersonation & safety

CPUNK is a permissionless ecosystem. Anyone can create accounts using our name, themes, or imagery.

  • Always double-check URLs and usernames before interacting.
  • CPUNK team members will never ask for your seed phrase, private keys, or remote access to your device.
  • When in doubt, verify information through the official Telegram and X accounts listed above.
