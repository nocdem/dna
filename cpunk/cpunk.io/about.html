About CPUNK

Privacy Is Not a Feature.
It's a Right.

CPUNK is building the infrastructure for a post-quantum world — where your identity, your messages, and your assets belong to you alone.

Our Mission

Preparing for the Post-Quantum Era

Modern encryption — RSA, ECC, and the algorithms that protect your bank account, your messages, and your identity — will be broken by quantum computers. This is not speculation. NIST has already published post-quantum cryptographic standards (FIPS 203, 204, 205) because the threat is real and the timeline is accelerating.

CPUNK doesn't wait for the quantum era to arrive. We build with these standards today. Our mission is simple: provide ordinary people with military-grade post-quantum privacy tools that are as easy to use as WhatsApp.

No PhD required. No command line. No key management headaches. Just open the app, create your identity, and communicate securely — knowing that your privacy is protected against both current and future threats.

Why Now?

Intelligence agencies and state actors are already executing "harvest now, decrypt later" attacks — collecting encrypted data today to decrypt it once quantum computers become powerful enough.

Every message you send with classical encryption is a message that can be read in the future. CPUNK ensures that your communications are quantum-safe from day one.

The Technology

One Seed, One Identity, One Ecosystem

A single BIP-39 seed phrase derives everything: your post-quantum identity (Dilithium5), your encryption keys (Kyber1024), and your multi-chain wallet addresses (Cellframe, Ethereum, Solana, TRON). One backup protects everything.

👤

DNA Identity

Self-sovereign, human-readable names tied to ML-DSA-87 key pairs. No phone number, no email address, no central authority. Your identity is yours — mathematically.

💬

DNA Connect

End-to-end encrypted messaging with zero metadata. Groups, channels, personal walls — all quantum-safe. Messages travel through a decentralized DHT, not corporate servers.

💰

DNA Wallet

Multi-chain wallet with DEX aggregation. Send tokens directly from chat. MEV-protected swaps across Cellframe, Ethereum, Solana, and TRON — all from one seed.

Why Post-Quantum?

The Clock Is Ticking

Quantum computers will break the encryption that protects the internet. Governments know this — that's why NIST spent 8 years selecting and standardizing post-quantum algorithms. CPUNK uses NIST Category 5 — the highest security level available — across every component.

The "harvest now, decrypt later" threat is not theoretical. Nation-states are collecting encrypted traffic today, stockpiling it for the day quantum computers can break it open. Every day you wait to switch to post-quantum encryption is another day your data is at risk.
